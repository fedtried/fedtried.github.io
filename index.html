About me

I am a final year electrical and electronic engineering student studying at the University of Manchester. My passion for technology stems from childhood and carried on as I continued my academic career.

The first coding language I attempted to learn was html and css for web development. I wanted to learn so I could modify aspects of my tumblr page when I was a teenager. I added in a couple of fancy features like a mouse trail and a music player but never learnt how powerful web development could be.

For my A-Levels I took Computer Science. Here is where I first learnt about algorithms and key coding concepts and techniques. I always enjoyed the problem solving aspects of starting a new project and figuring out the direction your code would take. I especially enjoyed the feeling when everything slipped into place and your program ran without any errors.

Later on in university, I had the opportunity to learn C and C++ through my modules and project work. I talk about my embedded systems project in my portfolio. This project was really key for my understanding of my entire degree as it tied together many theoretical concepts into a physical application.

Then comes COVID-19 and in April 2020 I started a Java Masterclass on Udemy. I had always wanted to learn Java due to its vast applications so I deciede to take a course. This course covered 80 hours worth of videos and covered everything right from the basics all the way to JavaFX and databases. I then took on a complete Web Developers bootcamp where I learnt the skills required for a web developer. This course is what gave me the technical foundations to create this website!

Currently, I am partaking in a C++ course to refresh my knowledge of that language. I hope to keep up the knowledge I have leant over the last couple of years and I hope you enjoy looking around my website

All the best, Federica
